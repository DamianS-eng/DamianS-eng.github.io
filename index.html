A bicycle, also known as a bike, is a human-powered vehicle that has two wheels, pedals, a chain or belt drive, and a handlebar. It is a popular form of transportation, sport, and leisure activity. Bicycles come in various types for different purposes, such as road bikes, mountain bikes, BMX bikes, and cruiser bikes. They are eco-friendly, energy-efficient, and promote physical fitness. Cycling is a relatively low-impact exercise that benefits people of all ages, from children riding around their neighborhoods to adults commuting to work or going on long rides for recreation.
